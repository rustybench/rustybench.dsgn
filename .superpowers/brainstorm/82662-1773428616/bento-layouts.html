Mobile Bento Grid Layouts

Which grid rhythm feels right for your portfolio? (Tap to select)

A

Tight & Compact

Pros

  • Shows more content above fold
  • Dynamic, busy energy
  • Modern editorial feel

Cons

  • Less breathing room
  • Harder to appreciate individual pieces
B

Balanced & Breathable

Pros

  • Good visual hierarchy
  • Pieces have space to breathe
  • Mix of hero & detail

Cons

  • Slightly less dense
  • More scrolling required
C

Airy & Editorial

Pros

  • Magazine-quality feel
  • Maximum breathing room
  • Hero pieces stand out

Cons

  • Least dense layout
  • Much more scrolling

Note: These use a 4-column grid for variety. Actual implementation would be optimized for mobile width with appropriate gaps and sizes.
